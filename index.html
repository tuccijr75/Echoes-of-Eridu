Echoes of Eridu is a myth-tech odyssey where Anunnaki law meets human defiance—obsidian nights lit by gold script, canals and ziggurats humming with power.

You are an Adamu runner, broker, and witness. Trade favors, track rumors, and wield sacred Names that bend local rules—at a risk that draws watchful audits.

Fight and race across land, sea, air, and space. Outmaneuver patrols, chart hidden routes, and outfit modular craft to survive storms, deserts, and star-road ambushes.

Begin aligned with one of five playable factions—each with its own ethos and progression—and let reputation, civility, and choices carve your legend through festival and war.
